Welcome to Level 6 of the Treasure Hunt Game!

In this level, you will explore a 10x10 grid to find the hidden treasure. Each cell represents a possible location for the treasure. Your task is to uncover the treasure by clicking on the cells. Not every cell contains the treasure, so use the clues wisely!

How to Play:

  • Grid Exploration: The game board consists of 100 cells arranged in a 10x10 grid. Click on any cell to explore it.
  • Clues and Feedback: After each click, you will receive a clue indicating your proximity to the treasure. Use these clues to narrow down the potential locations:
    • Move Left! You are within 1-2 cell of the treasure.
    • Move Right! You are within 2-3 cells of the treasure.
    • Move Up! You are within 3-4 cells of the treasure.
    • Move Down! You are more than 5 cells away from the treasure.
  • Score: Your score increases with each cell you explore. Aim to find the treasure with the fewest clicks to keep your score low.
  • Winning the Level: Find the cell containing the treasure to win the level. A congratulatory popup will appear with a video. Click the "Next Level" button in the popup to proceed to the next level.

Objective: Your goal is to find the hidden treasure with the fewest possible clicks. Pay attention to the clues to guide your search and improve your chances of success.

Good luck, treasure hunter! Start exploring the grid and find that treasure!
