Welcome to Level 5 of the Treasure Hunt Game!

In this level, you will explore a 9x9 grid to find the hidden treasure. Each cell represents a possible location for the treasure. Your task is to uncover the treasure by clicking on the cells. Not every cell contains the treasure, so use the clues wisely!

How to Play:

  • Grid Exploration: The game board consists of 81 cells arranged in a 9x9 grid. Click on any cell to explore it.
  • Clues and Feedback: After each click, you will receive a clue indicating your proximity to the treasure. Use these clues to narrow down the potential locations:
    • SpongeBob! You are within 1 cell of the treasure.
    • Mickey Mouse! You are within 2 cells of the treasure.
    • Bugs Bunny! You are within 3 cells of the treasure.
    • Tom & Jerry! You are within 4 cells of the treasure.
    • Doraemon! You are within 5 cells of the treasure.
    • shinchan! You are more than 6 cells away from the treasure.
  • Score: Your score increases with each cell you explore. Aim to find the treasure with the fewest clicks to keep your score low.
  • Winning the Level: Find the cell containing the treasure to win the level. A congratulatory popup will appear. Click the "Next Level" button in the popup to proceed to Level 6.

Objective: Your goal is to find the hidden treasure with the fewest possible clicks. Pay attention to the clues to guide your search and improve your chances of success.

Good luck, treasure hunter! Start exploring the grid and find that treasure!
